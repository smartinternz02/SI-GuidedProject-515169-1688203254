Welcome to Our Project!

Click on "open webcam" to view a series of images. We use the webcam to capture the user's expressions to the images and use Artificial Intelligence to detect the emotions displayed by the user. On the carousel page click "start model" once the webcam is ready. Click "stop model" to stop the slideshow. The various emotions displayed for each image is recorded and can be further used by downloading the result in the form of a csv file.
